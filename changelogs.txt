============================================================
COMING IN 1.6.0 - PLANNED BIG CHANGES
============================================================

ARENA SYSTEM OVERHAUL
  The Gladiator Society will no longer be accessible via comm relay.
  Instead, you will need to travel to a planet that has an Arena
  structure built on it. Arenas can appear on existing colony planets,
  or you can construct one yourself on your own colony.
  This makes the Society feel like a real place in the world rather
  than a floating menu.

GLADIATOR MERCENARY FACTION
  A proper Gladiator Society faction will be added to the game world.
  When a faction at war is about to send a fleet, there is a chance
  they first dispatch a Gladiator fleet as a vanguard - to soften up
  the target and test their defenses before committing their main force.
  This makes the Society an active participant in sector politics
  rather than just an arena operator.

Version 1.3.6 (hotfix):
- Dynamic Faction Detection: conflict-safe validation so only factions that can actually generate combat fleets are included.
- New config flags in `data/config/gs_factions.json`:
  - `validateUsingFleetGen` (bool): toggle validation via tiny test fleets.
  - `validationFleetType` (string): special fleet type used only for validation to avoid conflicts with other mods.
  - `vanillaModdedSplit` (0.0–1.0): controls vanilla vs modded selection chance (default 0.5 for equal chance).
- Validation fleets are hidden from gameplay and skip GS-specific interaction setup to reduce mod conflicts.
- Endless Mode uses the configurable vanilla/modded split; modded factions have the same chance to spawn as vanilla by default.
- Minor robustness: safer clamping and guards around config usage.

============================================================

!! REQUIRES A NEW GAME - NOT SAVE COMPATIBLE !!

NEW: Gladiator Society Faction
- The Gladiator Society is now a real faction in the sector
- Hegemony-origin lore: officially founded as a Hegemony security corps,
  evolved into an independent mercenary organization - still works primarily
  for the Hegemony but takes contracts from any faction willing to pay
- Uses Hegemony-derived tech (ships, weapons, blueprints)
- Appears in the Intel tab, Factions screen, and simulator like all other factions
- Fully integrated with Nexerelin: playable in game setup, participates in
  diplomacy, wars, colony expeditions, and all faction mechanics
- New faction logo: banner and crest
- Faction relationships: Favorable with Hegemony/Lions Guard, Hostile to Pirates/Remnants/Luddic Path

NEW: The Arena System
- New star system "The Arena" spawns at a fixed location in the sector
- Capital: Gladius Prime - terran planet, size 7, heavily militarized
  - Spaceport, Military Base, Ground Defenses, Heavy Industry, Farming
  - Open, storage, military, and black market submarkets
  - Commander Marcus Varro stationed here as quest contact
  - Planet description added
- The Arena station orbits Gladius Prime
  (all existing arena modes: Bounty, Endless Battle, Fleet Battles still accessible here)
- Comm Relay and Nav Buoy added to the system
- Two additional uninhabited planets with moons each (Ferrum, Scoria)
- Permanent GS Defense Force patrol fleet guards the system

NEW: Initiation Quest
- New quest "Gladiator Society: Initiation" unlocks the Salvage Pit
- Triggered by visiting Gladius Prime or The Arena station and speaking with Commander Varro
- Objective: destroy a Remnant patrol fleet in the system (scales to player fleet strength)
- Reward: credits, reputation boost, and permanent Salvage Pit access
- Quest tracked in the Intel tab with map marker

NEW: Salvage Pit
- Accessible via The Arena comm relay after completing the initiation quest
- Locked behind quest completion - shown as "[Locked] Salvage Pit" until unlocked
- Entry spawns a real Gate (with proper gate visuals) 400 units from the comm relay
- Uses hyperspace jump animation for immersive transition
- Entry gate removed after jumping in
- Procedurally generated system with:
  - 2-4 planets
  - 1-2 mining stations (always present)
  - 30% chance of research station
  - 40% chance of hab centers
  - Remnant fleets scaled to player fleet points (40-60%, clamped 40-250 FP), 3-5 fleets
  - Remnant Nexus battlestation
  - Navigation buoys and comm relays as objectives
  - 40% chance of rare derelict specials
  - Full salvage cache pool

Version 1.3.6 (hotfix):
- Dynamic Faction Detection: conflict-safe validation so only factions that can actually generate combat fleets are included.
- New config flags in `data/config/gs_factions.json`:
  - `validateUsingFleetGen` (bool): toggle validation via tiny test fleets.
  - `validationFleetType` (string): special fleet type used only for validation to avoid conflicts with other mods.
  - `vanillaModdedSplit` (0.0–1.0): controls vanilla vs modded selection chance (default 0.5 for equal chance).
- Validation fleets are hidden from gameplay and skip GS-specific interaction setup to reduce mod conflicts.
- Endless Mode uses the configurable vanilla/modded split; modded factions have the same chance to spawn as vanilla by default.
- Minor robustness: safer clamping and guards around config usage.

Version 1.3.5 (hotfix):

- Added safe faction fallbacks (pirates → independent) during fleet generation to increase robustness.
